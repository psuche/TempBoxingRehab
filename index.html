Do every morning or before boxing/footwork work

Lie on your back with feet on the wall, knees and hips at 90°...
Lie on back with feet on a low surface. Keep ribs down and alternate lowering...
Use a loop band around the big toe. Pull toward the heel without curling.
Front foot flat, back foot against the wall. Reach both arms forward as you descend.
Stay tall, ribs over pelvis. Quiet landings, opposite arm/leg sync.

📆 ALTERNATE DAY: GAIT + FOOTWORK DRILLS

Push off the big toe fully before stepping forward.
Exhale as the knee lifts. Opposite arm/leg for timing control.
Knees 1” off ground, hold with breath control and posture awareness.
Pause between each step to train control and balance.

🥋 BOXING PRACTICE (When Healthy)

Cues: heel-to-toe, big toe drive, weight shift, quiet landings.
Quick, quiet steps. Prioritize balance over speed.
Short bursts at fight pace with full breath recovery.
